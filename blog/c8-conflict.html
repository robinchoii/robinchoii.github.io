CONFLICTS

Discuss a time you had a conflict. What was it about? What happened?

Conflicts are inevitable so whenever I run into conflicts, I always try to find a solution to the conflict. The most recent conflict I had was with my roommate. We share a lot of the household items and food. However I always seems to be getting the short end of the stick. I always seems to be the one going out of my way to replace or get more of something.

What was the source of the conflict?

The source of this conflict is probably lack of communication which is actually the source for a lot of conflicts.

What basic emotions did you feel upon analyzing the conflict?

I felt very annoyed because something I just would think it is common sense and I shouldn't have to explain everything.

If you could go back, what would you do differently, if anything?

I would probably make a list or chart of the community things we use and switch off buying those items rather than assuming someone will get it. And maybe even separate our foods.

What did you learn from this experience?

I learned that communication is very important, in almost anything. I need to be on the right page with someone from the beginning so I know where they are coming from.
